What are We Doing

We are a non-profit organization aims to connect retirees from English Speaking countries with Chinese English Learners by online video. In the following month, languages including French and Germany will be added to our program.

Now?

Currently, O’range is connecting Chinese students with retirees from English speaking countries through internet video.

Why we found this?

For    Love,    Care,    Learn,    Communication   &   Reciprocity

Orange provides students an opportunity to speak with native speaker. Meanwhile, Orange helps retirees to realize their social value again and teach English.

How to join our Program

For the retirees,

1. Get on our website with Skype ID and sign up so that we can do better recommendation.

2. According to your available time and interests, we will recommend 3 students to you with their brief introduction.

3. You can choose one student only, by clicking the YES button. We will send the student notification email. Only after you click the YES button can the student see your Skype ID and other information. (privacy will be carefully protected)

4. Now the notification email has been sent to the student. Remember to check your Skype message box often and arrange chat time with the student！

For the students,

1. Sign in with your invitation code and Skype ID

2. We will notify you by email as soon as the match is available.

3. Remember to check the email box often

4. Please contact them and arrange the chat time by Skype message.

In the future

1. More languages!
